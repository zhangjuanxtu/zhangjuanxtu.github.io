Juan Zhang has been actively involved in undergraduate and graduate teaching, online course development, and student supervision at Xiangtan University.

## Courses

- Advanced Algebra, course coordinator and lead developer of the online course
- Foundations of Matrix Theory, online course available on Zhihuishu
- Mathematical Analysis, core member of the national first-class undergraduate course team

## Teaching Leadership

- Director of the Advanced Algebra Course Center at Xiangtan University
- Principal investigator of key education-reform projects supported by the China Association of Higher Education and by Hunan Province
- During her service as department chair, the Mathematics and Applied Mathematics program was selected as a national first-class undergraduate major

## Student Supervision

- Supervised 7 doctoral students and 25 master's students
- Students have received national scholarships, the Hunan Outstanding Master's Thesis award, Xiangtan University President's Special Award, and recognition in algorithm and performance optimization competitions
