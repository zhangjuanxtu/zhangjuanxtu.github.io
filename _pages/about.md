Juan Zhang is a Professor of Mathematics and PhD supervisor at Xiangtan University. She serves as Vice Dean of the School of Mathematics and Computational Science and Executive Deputy Director of the Ministry of Education Key Laboratory of Intelligent Computing and Information Processing.

Her research centers on numerical linear algebra, matrix computation, optimization, and AI for mathematics. She has published more than twenty SCI papers in leading journals such as SIAM Journal on Numerical Analysis, SIAM Journal on Scientific Computing, Automatica, IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering, Journal of Computational Physics, and Journal of Scientific Computing.

She has led or contributed to more than ten national and provincial projects, including grants from the National Natural Science Foundation of China and the China Postdoctoral Science Foundation.

## Research

- Numerical linear algebra and matrix computations
- Linear systems, matrix equations, and optimization algorithms
- Control theory and AI for mathematics
