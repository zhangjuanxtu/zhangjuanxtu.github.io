Juan Zhang is a Professor of Mathematics and PhD supervisor in the School of Mathematics and Computational Science at Xiangtan University. She currently serves as Vice Dean of the School and Executive Deputy Director of the Ministry of Education Key Laboratory of Intelligent Computing and Information Processing. She has held visiting appointments at the National University of Singapore and the University of Macau.

Her research focuses on numerical linear algebra, control theory, optimization algorithms, matrix computation, linear system solving, matrix optimization, and AI for mathematics. Over the past five years, she has published more than twenty SCI papers in journals including SIAM Journal on Numerical Analysis, SIAM Journal on Scientific Computing, Automatica, IEEE Transactions on Knowledge and Data Engineering, Journal of Computational Physics, Journal of Scientific Computing, and CSIAM Transactions on Applied Mathematics.

## Research Interests

- Numerical linear algebra and matrix computations
- Linear systems and matrix equations
- Control theory and optimization algorithms
- AI for mathematics
