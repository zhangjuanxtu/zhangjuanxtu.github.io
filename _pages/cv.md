## Research

- Numerical linear algebra and matrix computations
- Iterative methods, preconditioning, and matrix equations
- Data-driven parameter prediction for algebraic systems
- Scientific computing at the intersection of optimization and AI

## Education

- MS in Operations Research and Cybernetics, Xiangtan University, 2006-2009
- BS in Information and Computing Science, Xiangtan University, 2002-2006

## Appointments
